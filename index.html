ACEP Demonstration Video

This video showcases the performance of the Autonomous Cell Exploration Planner (ACEP) in a simulated robotic scenario. Using a 3D sensor mounted on a robotic manipulator, the ACEP framework autonomously explores and maps unknown environments.
